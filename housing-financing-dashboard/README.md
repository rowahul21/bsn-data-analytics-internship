# 🏠 Housing Financing Ecosystem Dashboard (2021–2025)

## 📊 Overview
This dashboard is designed to analyze customer characteristics in housing financing (KPR) to support more targeted product development and strategic evaluation.

## 🎯 Objective
To provide a comprehensive view of customer demographics, financial behavior, and housing preferences, enabling management to design more relevant financial products and improve decision-making.

## 👥 Users
- Bank Management
- Branch Offices

## 📂 Data Source
- Internal Bank Data
- Data types:
  - Categorical data
  - Transaction data
  - Customer demographics
  - Financing realization

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills
- Google Looker Studio
- SQL
- Python
- Excel / Spreadsheet
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

## 📈 Key Insights
- 65% of housing loan customers are male
- Majority occupations: administrative workers and entrepreneurs
- Education level dominated by high school graduates (59%), followed by bachelor’s degree (25%)
- Credit collectability is mostly **current (lancar)**, especially among fixed-income customers
- Customer base is dominated by Millennials, followed by Gen Z and Gen X
- Majority income range: IDR 0–5 million/month
- Most selected loan tenor: 10–15 years
- Most popular house types: 21–36 m²
- Highest concentration of customers in West Java, especially Bogor and Bekasi

## 💡 Business Value
This dashboard helps identify customer segmentation patterns and risk profiles, enabling more precise targeting strategies and improving financing product alignment with market demand.
